What is McRaven Tour Home?

Originally built in 1797, McRaven House remains one of the most popular attractions in the state of Mississippi. With its 200-year history, McRaven has come to be known as "The Time Capsule of the South" according to National Geographic Magazine, and has no shortage of pre-civil war to post-civil war artifacts and architecture.
McRaven also harbors a frightening past. It has come to be known as "The Most Haunted House in Mississippi." Click here to learn more about the inhabitants that never truly left this beautiful home.
