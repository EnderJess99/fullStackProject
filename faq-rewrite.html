Frequently Asked Questions:

- Do I need to make a reservation?

Nope! No reservations are required for our history or haunted tours. The only ticket in which a reservation is necessary is our investigation tickets, as these are on set dates. However, keep in mind that our haunted tours are only given on Fridays, Saturdays, and Sundays at 7pm and 830pm exactly.

- Are you a Bed and Breakfast?

No, McRaven is not a bed and breakfast, just a tour home.

- Can we bring our own equipment for investigations?

Yes! You are welcome to bring your own ghost equipment to the house to investigate.

- Can we take pictures?

Absolutely! We encourage pictures of the house. We only ask that there is no use of flash photography inside of the home.

- Can we take a video?

Yes and No. We ask that there is no video or audio taken of the tour itself. However, should you wish to make small videos (for example, when something strange happens in the house) then you are welcome. Once again, please be mindful not to make any recording of the tour.
